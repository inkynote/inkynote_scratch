Dark Moonlight

Posted on: [Date]

Category: poem

Mundane life, I gaze at the crescent moon,

Petals falling in silence, a gentle tune.

Empathy, I cradle a butterfly’s grace,

As it fades, memories swirl, a watery place.

I've seen blossoms in words, lost in a trance,

Beneath a red roof, broken by chance.

Embracing my fears, I watch time glide,

Once trusted, now lonely, with nothing to hide.

Sincerity shines like the flawless moon's glow,

Flowers whisper secrets only they know.

Happiness found in the simplest of ways,

Moonlight, my armor through all of my days.

Admiring the scars, I strive to be kind,

To love what I once pushed far from my mind.

Mundane life is brief, yet dreams stretch so long,

In the heart of the night, they sing their sweet song.
